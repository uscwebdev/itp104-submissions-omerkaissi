Scope

This website is designed as a one-page layout, featuring several comprehensive sections. Each section has its unique purpose. We start with "Coffee Types", delving into the different varieties of coffee beans. Following that, we have "Brewing Methods" which provides insights into the diverse ways to brew the perfect cup. The "Coffee Recipes" section introduces a collection of popular coffee beverages and how to make them. "Reviews & Recommendations" is dedicated to feedback on various coffee brands and products. Finally, we have a "Contact & Feedback" section for users to get in touch or provide their comments.
